## The Learning Landscape Changed

AI tools have fundamentally changed how we learn new skills. Instead of searching through documentation for hours, you can now get instant, contextual answers.

## Three AI-Powered Learning Strategies

### 1. Use AI as Your Personal Tutor

Ask an AI assistant to explain complex topics at your level. Prompt it with: "Explain X to me like I'm a beginner who knows Y."

### 2. Get Instant Feedback on Your Work

Whether you're coding, writing, or designing, AI can review your output and suggest improvements in real time.

### 3. Generate Practice Problems

Tell an AI what you're learning and ask it to create custom exercises. This beats generic textbook problems every time.
